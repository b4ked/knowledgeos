# Getting Started with KnowledgeOS

KnowledgeOS is a personal knowledge management tool that helps you capture, connect, and compile your notes into structured wiki articles.

## How it works

1. Write raw notes in the **Raw** folder — these are your source material, rough notes, meeting notes, research, anything.
2. Select one or more raw notes and click **Compile Selected** — the AI compiles them into a structured wiki note with [[wikilinks]] connecting concepts.
3. Browse your wiki in the **Wiki** folder — compiled notes form a connected knowledge graph.
4. Use **Chat** to ask questions across your entire knowledge base using RAG.

## Key concepts

- **Raw notes** are input. They can be messy, partial, or exploratory.
- **Wiki notes** are output. They are structured, linked, and searchable.
- **Wikilinks** like [[Knowledge Graph]] and [[Personal Knowledge Management]] connect ideas across your vault.
- **Compile** uses an LLM to transform raw material into structured wiki format.
