# Welcome to KnowledgeOS

## Overview

[[KnowledgeOS]] is a personal knowledge management system that bridges raw note-taking with structured [[Knowledge Graph]] construction. It uses an LLM to compile source material into interconnected wiki notes, enabling a self-organising [[Second Brain]].

## Key Concepts

- [[Raw Notes]] — unstructured source material and research input
- [[Wiki Notes]] — compiled, structured output with wikilinks
- [[Compilation]] — the AI-powered transformation from raw to wiki format
- [[Wikilinks]] — connections between concepts that build the knowledge graph
- [[RAG]] — retrieval-augmented generation for querying your knowledge base
- [[Knowledge Graph]] — the emergent network of connected ideas

## How to use this vault

1. Switch to the **Raw** tab and explore the sample raw note
2. Check the note checkbox and click **Compile Selected**
3. View the compiled result in the **Wiki** tab
4. Open the **Graph** view to see your knowledge graph
5. Open **Chat** to ask questions across your notes

## Connections

This vault demonstrates a minimal working example of [[Personal Knowledge Management]] with AI-assisted [[Knowledge Synthesis]]. It connects ideas from [[Note-Taking]], [[Zettelkasten]], and [[Spaced Repetition]] traditions.
